So I always thought it was a shame that all the fancy DLC variant blocks didn't increase block power in some way shape or form. Needs to be fair for everyone, I know. But I want these fancy looking blocks to actually be fancy. And this mod does exactly that!

I went through the various DLC packs and grabbed thrusters, cargo cans, batteries, etc... anything that was just a visual update to an existing block that had a function - and I buffed the stats. The names of all the buffed blocks remain the same for the most part, and the blocks themselves do not change. All this mod is doing in changing some data around. So if you install the mod, your DLC blocks won't vanish. And if you remove the mod, your DLC blocks still wont vanish.

[quote]
Ok, so what did I actually do...
[list]
[*]Any block with the prefix [u]Upgraded[/u] is more or less a x2 version of its base game counterpart
[*]Any block with the prefix [u]Advanced[/u] is a x3 version of its base game counterpart
[*]Component costs were doubled for x2 blocks and tripled for x3 blocks
[*]A Superconductor tax was added to all impacted blocks. The amount scales with block size:
[list]
[*]L-grid Large block: 10 SC
[*]L-grid Small block: 5 SC
[*]S-grid Large block: 4 SC
[*]S-grid Small block: 2 SC
[/list]
[*]Advanced blocks also get a Reactor Component tax on top (5 / 2 / 2 / 1) - this gates them behind Platinum
[*]Various stats like thrust, cargo capacity, refine speed, etc... were either x2 or x3
[*]All blocks got a durability buff with the added components
[*]Power output and consumption are unchanged for most blocks. Exceptions:
[list]
[*]Warfare Reactors produce 2x power AND consume half the fuel per MW. Each Uranium ingot lasts 4x longer than vanilla. Heavier SC tax (200 / 100 / 20 / 5) to balance.
[*]Sci-Fi Hydrogen Engine has 2x fuel capacity and half H2 consumption per MW. 4x total runtime on a full tank.
[*]Twin Blade Wind Turbine produces 2x the power of vanilla.
[/list]
[*]For weapons, damage was not increased
[list]
[*]Weapons do benefit from increased durability and internal cargo space though, so they last longer and can shoot more if they get disconnected from your cargo grid
[*]Turrets specifically got a small range buff
[/list]
[*]For ore detectors, the range increase made some assumptions
[list]
[*]The ranges on these assume you have already buffed up the base ore detector
[*]If your base LG ore detector has a 1km range, and the SG one has a 300m range, this update should make sense
[/list]
[*]Thrusters  were figured out a little differently
[list]
[*]Thruster material cost was increased to 2x and 3x base thruster cost for upgraded and advanced respectively
[*]The thrust values were set by attempting to sort out the thrust to mass ratio. Upgraded thrusters should have roughly 2x the thrust to mass ratio of vanilla thrusters, and advanced should have roughly 3x
[*]Advanced Flat Atmospheric Thrusters specifically were rebenchmarked against vanilla [u]standard[/u] atmo thrusters, not vanilla flat. Significantly stronger than a naive 3x of "flat" alone would be.
[*]Prototech thrusters still have the best thrust to mass ratio for ion thrusters
[/list]
[/list]
[/quote]
